About me

Deborah Plana, BS, is an MD–PhD student in the Harvard‐MIT Health Sciences and Technology program. She is currently pursuing her PhD in the Harvard Systems Biology program, jointly supervised by Peter Sorger, PhD, from Harvard Medical School and Adam Palmer, PhD, from the UNC School of Medicine.

She previously earned her undergraduate degree in Biological Engineering at MIT with a minor in Statistics and Data Science, working in the lab of Douglas Lauffenburger. Her research interests include cancer systems biology and clinical trials in oncology.
